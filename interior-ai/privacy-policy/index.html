Summary

This policy explains what information we collect in the App, why we collect it, and how you can request deletion or get help. If you have questions, contact us at support@9dogs.com.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Photos and images you choose to upload (for example, room photos) to generate outputs inside the App.
  • Support messages you send to us (for example, email content and attachments).

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information (such as device model, OS version, app version, language, and approximate region).
  • Diagnostics (crash logs and performance data) to help us fix bugs and improve reliability.
  • Usage data (for example, which screens are visited and which features are used) in aggregated or de-identified form where possible.

Note: The exact categories may vary depending on the features you use and how the App is configured.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the App (including generating outputs from your inputs).
  • Improve features, usability, and performance.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Communicate with you about support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We may share information in these limited cases:

  • Service providers that help us run the App (for example, hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and customer support). They are permitted to process data only to provide services to us.
  • Legal and safety when we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with law or protect rights, safety, and integrity.
  • Business transfers if we’re involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (you’ll be notified if required by law).

Your choices

  • Access and deletion requests: You can contact us to request access to or deletion of personal information we may hold about you.
  • Permissions: You can control photo/library permissions in your device settings.
  • Analytics controls: If the App provides an in-app toggle for analytics, you can use it to opt out.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We may retain de-identified or aggregated data longer.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we can’t guarantee absolute security.

Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the age defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when we make changes. Material changes may be communicated in the App or by other means where appropriate.
